# Card Illustration Prompts

These prompts are for the first authored `Blood Rogue` combat card art batch.

Target output:
- vertical card illustration for a dark-fantasy deckbuilder
- readable at small size inside a framed card window
- single subject or tight vignette
- no text, no logo, no border, no UI
- dark background or transparent-style negative space around the subject
- premium painterly gothic style, not cartoon, not comic-book flat

Suggested output files:
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_magic_arrow.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_fire_arrow.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_inner_sight.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_dodge.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_jab.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/amazon_critical_strike.png`
- `assets/curated/card-illustrations/rally_mercenary.png`

Use this shared addendum on every prompt:

```text
Create a premium dark-fantasy card illustration for an original game called Blood Rogue.

Output requirements:
Single vertical illustration only.
Portrait orientation.
High-resolution PNG.
No text, no logo, no frame, no watermark.
The subject must be centered and readable inside a small card art window.
Keep the edges dark and uncluttered so the art survives being cropped into a card.

Art direction:
Painterly gothic dark fantasy.
Black iron, old parchment, dried blood, ember light, smoke, shadow, worn leather, and muted occult glow.
Elegant and severe rather than flashy.
This should feel like premium card art for a mature deckbuilder, not an action icon.

Composition:
One strong focal subject.
Minimal background clutter.
Clear silhouette.
Readable at thumbnail size.

Avoid:
No modern objects.
No bright cartoon colors.
No anime look.
No exaggerated comic posing.
No in-image captions.
```
