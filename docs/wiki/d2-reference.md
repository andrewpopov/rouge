## NPCs by Act

### Act I — The Sightless Eye (Rogue Encampment)

| NPC | Role | Services | Description |
|-----|------|----------|-------------|
| **Akara** | Rogue Priestess | Heals; sells staves, scepters, wands, tomes, scrolls, keys, potions | Formal, intoning rather than speaking. Naturally suspicious of outsiders but needs your help. |
| **Charsi** | Blacksmith | Repairs; sells armor, weapons, bolts, arrows | Somewhat slow-witted simple country gal, but anger enters her voice when she mentions demons. |
| **Deckard Cain** | Horadrim Sage | Identifies items (100g fee if rescued by Rogues) | Schooled in the most arcane history and lore. Trapped in Tristram initially. |
| **Flavie** | Rogue Scout | None | Warns of dangers beyond the Den of Evil. |
| **Gheed** | Traveling Merchant | Sells armor, weapons, bolts, arrows, keys; gambling | Speech reeks of flowers. |
| **Kashya** | Mercenary Captain | Hires Rogue mercenaries (after Blood Raven or level 9) | Hard-as-nails. Doesn't shy from talking about blood and gore. Impatient and curt until you win her trust. |
| **Warriv** | Caravan Leader | Travel to Act II after completion | Tall, Arabic-looking, a dour adage-sayer. |

**Subpage:** https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/npcs/act1.shtml

### Act II — The Secret of the Vizjerei (Lut Gholein)

| NPC | Role | Services | Description |
|-----|------|----------|-------------|
| **Atma** | Tavern Owner | Heals (free) | Comely woman who recently lost her family. Consumed by grief and hatred, seeking vengeance. |
| **Drognan** | Wizard | Sells staves, scepters, wands, tomes, scrolls, potions | Man of few and private words. Not many have even heard him speak. |
| **Elzix** | Innkeeper (ex-bandit) | Sells armor, weapons; gambling | Once a bandit king who lost his left eye. Traded secrets for freedom. |
| **Fara** | Retired Paladin | Heals; repairs; sells armor, weapons | Once a Holy warrior for the Church of Light. Departed due to institutional corruption. |
| **Geglash** | Town Drunk | None | Fearless, drunken blowhard with an irrational fear of water and ships. |
| **Greiz** | Guard Captain | Hires mercenaries | Aggressive enforcer. Takes crap from no one. |
| **Jerhyn** | Town Leader | None | Young leader trying in vain to devise a strategy against evil. |
| **Kaelan** | Palace Guard | None | Guards Jerhyn's palace entrance. |
| **Lysander** | Alchemist | Sells keys, potions | Short-tempered and nearly deaf from explosive ingredient mixing. |
| **Meshif** | Ship Captain | Travel east to Act III | Friendly merchant motivated by gold, gems, and jade statuettes. |
| **Warriv** | Caravan Operator | Travel west to Act I | Large man with a stern demeanor who became friendlier after passage reopened. |

**Subpage:** https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/npcs/act2.shtml

### Act III — The Infernal Gate (Kurast Docks)

| NPC | Role | Services | Description |
|-----|------|----------|-------------|
| **Alkor** | Potion Dealer | Sells potions; gambling | Pleasant but distracted, always reading a book and mumbling strange words. |
| **Asheara** | Iron Wolves Leader | Hires Iron Wolf mercenaries; sells armor, weapons | Tough but friendly. Participates in combat and tavern activities with her comrades. |
| **Hratli** | Sorcerer-Blacksmith | Repairs; sells armor, weapons, keys | The mad poet who melds metal with magic. Sharp and quick tongue. Charges premium prices. |
| **Meshif** | Sailor | Travel west to Act II | Affected by Kurast's decline. Considers the city his home-away-from-home. |
| **Natalya** | Assassin | None | Operating on a mercenary mission. |
| **Ormus** | Mage-Poet | Heals (free); sells armor, staves, wands, scepters, tomes, potions, scrolls | The mad poet who speaks in rhymes and riddles. Member of a magi order. |

**Subpage:** https://classic.battle.net/diablo2exp/npcs/act3.shtml

### Act V — Lord of Destruction (Harrogath)

| NPC | Role | Services | Description |
