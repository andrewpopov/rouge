## Identity

Freeze and slow enemies into harmlessness, then burst them down. The safest Sorceress lane through superior crowd control. Disruption first, damage second.

## Key Cards

- ice_bolt → ice_blast → glacial_spike — Single-target cold chain
- frozen_armor / shiver_armor / chilling_armor — Defensive cold auras
- frost_nova — AoE freeze
- blizzard — AoE cold damage
- frozen_orb — Ultimate cold payoff
- cold_mastery — Cold damage amplifier

## Synergy Loop

1. Freeze/slow enemies with cold spells (frost_nova, glacial_spike)
2. Frozen enemies can't attack — free damage window
3. Cold mastery amplifies all cold damage
4. Blizzard/frozen_orb deal massive AoE while enemies are locked down
5. Lightning support adds damage variety against cold-resistant targets

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Staves with freeze affix
- **Crossbows** not available (Sorceress locked to Staves) — get freeze from skills instead

## Boss Strategy

Freeze-lock boss when possible. Between freeze windows, stack cold mastery for burst. Against cold-immune bosses, need lightning support damage.

## Failure Modes

- Cold-immune enemies completely shut down the primary plan
- Without lightning support, no damage against resistant targets
- Setup time needed — fast openers can kill before freeze is online
