## Identity

Fire and cold elemental magic. Setup burn/slow conditions, then trigger burst payoffs. AoE pressure scales over the fight as conditions stack.

## Key Cards

- firestorm — Fire setup
- arctic_blast — Cold setup
- fissure — Fire AoE payoff
- twister — Wind disruption
- cyclone_armor — Defensive elemental shield
- volcano, hurricane, armageddon — Ultimate AoE payoffs

## Synergy Loop

1. Apply burn with fire spells (firestorm, fissure)
2. Apply slow with cold spells (arctic_blast)
3. Conditions stack, increasing payoff card damage
4. AoE ultimates (hurricane, armageddon) benefit from stacked conditions
5. Summoning support provides bodies while setting up

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Staves preferred (element affixes). Leaf runeword (Tir+Ral) for fire: +5 burn
- **Staves** offer burn/freeze/shock (1 each) for element-specific scaling

## Boss Strategy

Get conditions running early. Burn stacking creates a clock — boss takes increasing damage each turn. Need enough defense to survive while conditions pay off.

## Failure Modes

- Needs setup time — weak against fast openers (AMBUSH_OPENING, VANGUARD_RUSH)
- AoE focus can over-invest in clearing trash instead of boss damage
- Without summoning support, lacks bodies to absorb hits during setup
