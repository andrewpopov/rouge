## Identity

Faster and riskier Amazon lane built around aggressive tempo swings. Highest upside and highest punish window. Lightning-flavored burst with paralyze chains.

## Synergy Loop

1. Power_strike (11 + 2 paralyze) and charged_strike (12x2 + 3 paralyze) apply paralyze
2. Storm_javelin deals +8 if target paralyzed
3. Lightning_fury deals 13 AoE + 3 paralyze to all
4. Javelin_mastery aura gives javelin cards +3 dmg +1 paralyze
5. Paralyze → payoff → more paralyze chain reaction

## Evolution Chains

- jab → power_strike → charged_strike → lightning_fury

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Javelin or Spear with shock affixes. Crescent Moon runeword (Shael+Um+Tir) for +8 dmg
- **Armor:** Survival-focused — this build takes more hits during committed attack turns

## Boss Strategy

Draft enough guard or tempo support that burst turns don't turn into suicide. This build can spike bosses in 2-3 turns but is vulnerable during commit windows.

## Failure Modes

- Overcommits and dies to boss pressure on burst turns
- No guard/healing to survive counter-attacks
- Paralyzed enemies still dangerous if not finished
