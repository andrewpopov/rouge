## Identity

Build charges through setup attacks, then release them in explosive finishers. Elemental combos (fire, cold, lightning) provide damage variety. Every dead card in the deck breaks combo sequencing.

## Key Cards

- tiger_strike — Charge builder, elemental damage
- dragon_talon, dragon_claw — Charge consumers, multi-hit
- cobra_strike — Life/mana steal on charge release
- dragon_tail — AoE charge release
- dragon_flight — Mobility + charge release

## Synergy Loop

1. Play charge-building attacks (tiger_strike, cobra_strike)
2. Release charges with finishers (dragon_claw, dragon_tail)
3. Shadow discipline support provides survivability between combos
4. Tight deck ensures charge → release → charge cycle every 2-3 turns

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Swords — Assassin is locked to sword family
- **Pattern runeword** (Ort+Dol) for swords: +6 dmg, +4 guard, +2 energy, +6 life

## Boss Strategy

Time charge releases for maximum burst. Use shadow discipline defensively between burst windows. Don't overcommit charges when boss is about to telegraphed attack.

## Failure Modes

- Combo sequencing broken by deck bloat or bad draw
- Struggles against swarms — single-target focused
- Needs shadow support for defense, which costs deck slots
