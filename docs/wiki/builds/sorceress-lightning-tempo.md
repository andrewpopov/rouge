## Identity

Most flexible Sorceress build. Sustained pressure through chain lightning, utility through teleport and static field, disruption through nova and thunder storm. Can pivot between damage and utility.

## Key Cards

- charged_bolt → lightning → chain_lightning — Damage chain
- static_field — Percentage-based damage (scales with enemy HP)
- telekinesis — Utility
- nova — AoE burst
- teleport — Repositioning / emergency escape
- thunder_storm — Persistent lightning damage
- energy_shield — Damage → mana conversion (synergizes with 35 ENE)
- lightning_mastery — Lightning damage amplifier

## Synergy Loop

1. Static Field deals percentage-based damage (huge vs bosses)
2. Chain Lightning hits multiple enemies for sustained pressure
3. Thunder Storm deals passive damage each turn
4. Energy Shield converts damage to mana (35 ENE = large mana pool)
5. Cold support (freeze/slow) provides crowd control
6. Teleport provides emergency repositioning

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Staves with shock affix
- **Energy-boosting equipment** synergizes with Energy Shield

## Boss Strategy

Static Field deals percentage damage — most effective in early boss phases. Transition to chain lightning for sustained output. Energy Shield + high ENE provides survivability.

## Failure Modes

- Lightning-immune enemies need cold support damage
- Static Field becomes less effective as boss HP drops
- Energy Shield drains mana — can run dry without Warmth or energy equipment
