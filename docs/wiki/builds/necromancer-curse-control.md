## Identity

Debuff-focused support playstyle. Curses weaken enemies while allies (mercenary, summons) do the killing. The most hybrid-friendly Necromancer lane — can splash from any tree.

## Key Cards

- amplify_damage — Increase enemy damage taken
- dim_vision — Reduce enemy effectiveness
- weaken — Reduce enemy damage output
- terror — Fear-based disruption
- confuse — Enemy confusion/friendly fire
- attract — Redirect enemy targeting
- decrepify — Ultimate debuff combination
- lower_resist — Strip enemy resistances

## Synergy Loop

1. Apply curses to weaken enemies (amplify_damage, weaken)
2. Mercenary and summons deal amplified damage to cursed enemies
3. Mass curses affect all enemies simultaneously
4. Lower_resist strips elemental defenses for poison/bone support
5. Curse uptime is the engine — one curse per turn keeps value flowing

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Wands (locked family). White runeword (Dol+Io): +5 dmg, +3 guard, +2 merc
- **Wand affixes:** Slow (2), Curses proficiency

## Boss Strategy

Amplify_damage on boss increases all incoming damage from all sources. Stack curses and let mercenary/summons carry the damage. Safest Necromancer boss approach.

## Failure Modes

- Curse alone doesn't kill — needs damage sources (merc, summons, bone)
- Over-investing in curses with no offensive output leads to stalls
- Single-target curses need careful targeting to maximize value
