## Identity

The safest Assassin lane. Uses burst of speed, cloak of shadows, fade, and weapon block for near-permanent defensive uptime. Shadow Warrior and Shadow Master provide persistent ally damage. Most hybrid-friendly — can splash utility from other trees.

## Key Cards

- burst_of_speed — Action speed buff
- cloak_of_shadows — Disruption + defense
- fade — Damage reduction
- weapon_block — Incoming attack mitigation
- shadow_warrior, shadow_master — Persistent shadow allies
- mind_blast — Enemy disruption

## Synergy Loop

1. Defensive buffs (fade, weapon_block) reduce incoming damage
2. Shadow summons deal passive damage while hero defends
3. Disruption (mind_blast, cloak) prevents enemy burst turns
4. Cycle between defense and disruption, letting shadows do the killing

## Boss Strategy

Outlast through superior mitigation. Shadow allies provide damage while hero focuses on survival. Long fights favor this build.

## Failure Modes

- Can stall against high-HP bosses if damage is insufficient
- Needs at least some offensive cards to close fights
- Shadow summons can die to AoE, losing the primary damage source
