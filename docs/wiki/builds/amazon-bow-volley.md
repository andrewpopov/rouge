## Identity

Repeated ranged attacks, cleaner draw texture, and reliable pressure over explosive spikes. The "most stable" Amazon lane — wins through consistency rather than burst.

## Synergy Loop

1. Inner_sight applies slow + marks merc for +8, generating value every play
2. Cold_arrow and multiple_shot apply slow across enemies
3. Penetrate deals 10 dmg + marks merc +10 on slowed targets
4. Guided_arrow (16 dmg + draw) and pierce (26 dmg + merc +18) are finishers
5. Arrow_mastery aura gives all ranged cards +3 damage

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Bow or Crossbow progression. Edge runeword (Tal+Amn+El) is best-in-slot (+8 dmg, +2 burn)
- **Armor:** Stealth (Tal+Eth) for early survival, Smoke (Tal+Eth+Lum) for mid-game
- **Crossbows** add freeze on hit — strong synergy with slow-based control

## Boss Strategy

Survive long enough to line up repeated ranged payoff turns. Don't try to one-cycle bosses — this build wins through sustained pressure over 5-8 turns. Draft guard and healing cards for boss burst windows.

## Failure Modes

- Too little defense before payoff turns
- Deck bloats above 17 cards with off-spec neutral filler
- No answer for high-guard enemies without sunder

## Unwanted Cards

swing, measured_swing, kick, mark_target — neutral filler that dilutes the deck.
