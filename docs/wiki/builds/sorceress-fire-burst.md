## Identity

Pure damage output. Burn stacking creates a ticking clock on every enemy. Fire Mastery amplifies all fire damage. Meteor and Hydra provide ultimate payoffs. Highest damage ceiling of any Sorceress build.

## Key Cards

- fire_bolt → fire_ball → meteor — Single-target fire chain
- inferno, blaze — Sustained fire DoT
- fire_wall — Persistent fire zone
- warmth — Energy recovery (enables more spell casts)
- enchant — Buff mercenary with fire damage
- fire_mastery — Fire damage amplifier
- hydra — Persistent fire summon

## Synergy Loop

1. Apply burn stacks through fire spells
2. Burn deals damage every turn, scaling with stacks
3. Fire Mastery amplifies all fire damage and burn
4. Warmth provides energy recovery for sustained casting
5. Meteor/Hydra as ultimate payoffs after burn stacks

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Staves with burn affix. **Leaf runeword (Tir+Ral): +5 burn** — exceptional for this build
- **heroBurnBonus** from equipment directly scales the build

## Boss Strategy

Stack burn early, let it tick. Fire Mastery + burn stacking means boss takes increasing damage each turn. Need enough defense to survive while burn pays off — lowest HP class.

## Failure Modes

- Needs the fight to last long enough for burn to pay off
- Fire-immune enemies shut down the entire build (no support tree)
- Lowest HP (40) — dies to unmitigated burst before burn value accumulates
- No support tree — most all-in of any Sorceress build
