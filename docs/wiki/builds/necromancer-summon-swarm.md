## Identity

Raise a persistent undead army. Skeletons, skeletal mages, and golems overwhelm through numbers. Skeleton Mastery buffs all undead. The Necromancer hides behind their army.

## Key Cards

- raise_skeleton — Basic summon (core)
- skeleton_mastery — Buffs all skeletons
- clay_golem → blood_golem → iron_golem → fire_golem — Golem progression
- golem_mastery — Buffs all golems
- raise_skeletal_mage — Ranged summon variant
- revive — Resurrect dead enemies as allies

## Synergy Loop

1. Raise skeletons early for board presence
2. Skeleton Mastery amplifies all skeleton stats
3. Golem provides tank/anchor for the army
4. Golem Mastery buffs golem survivability
5. Curse support (amplify_damage) increases all summon damage
6. Army absorbs hits meant for the fragile Necromancer

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Wands. Summon quality matters more than weapon damage
- **Focus on hero survivability** — the army does the damage

## Boss Strategy

Full summon board before boss encounter. Army absorbs boss attacks while dealing passive damage. Replace fallen summons during the fight. Curse support amplifies army's output.

## Failure Modes

- AoE enemy attacks clear the army (board wipe)
- Early investment required — slow to build, vulnerable before army establishes
- Need enough summon cards to rebuild after losses
- Lowest HP class — if army falls, hero is extremely exposed
