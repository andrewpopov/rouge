## Identity

Most consistent solo damage output of any Necromancer build. Bone spells deal magic damage (unresistable). Corpse Explosion chains off dead enemies for escalating AoE.

## Key Cards

- teeth — Basic bone AoE
- bone_armor — Defensive bone shield
- corpse_explosion — Chain-reaction AoE off corpses
- bone_spear — Direct magic damage
- bone_wall, bone_prison — Utility/defense
- poison_nova — Poison AoE burst
- bone_spirit — Homing magic damage finisher

## Synergy Loop

1. Bone spells deal magic damage (ignores resistances)
2. Kill one enemy → Corpse Explosion chains off the body
3. More kills → more corpses → more explosions (snowball)
4. Curse support (amplify_damage) increases all bone damage
5. Poison_nova adds DoT pressure alongside bone burst

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Wands. White runeword (Dol+Io) for general use
- **Magic damage ignores resistances** — no need for element-specific weapon affixes

## Boss Strategy

Direct bone spell damage against boss. No corpse explosion in single-target boss fights (no adds), so need pure bone spell damage output. Curse support amplifies boss damage taken.

## Failure Modes

- Boss fights without adds lack corpse explosion chains
- Needs curse support to amplify damage on resistant targets
- Lowest HP class — bone_armor/bone_wall essential for survival
