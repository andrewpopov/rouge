## Identity

The most stable Barbarian path. Reliable weapon scaling and sturdy draw patterns. Outlasts awkward hands and keeps pressure consistent. Fewer ceiling moments but fewer floor moments too.

## Key Cards

- sword_mastery / axe_mastery / mace_mastery — Weapon scaling passives
- weapon_mastery — Universal weapon buff
- iron_skin → steel_skin — Guard scaling
- natural_resistance → unyielding — Resistance/defense
- increased_stamina, increased_speed — Efficiency passives

## Synergy Loop

1. Mastery passives buff every weapon attack without costing energy
2. Weapon scaling compounds with good weapon progression
3. Iron_skin/natural_resistance provide reliable defense each turn
4. Fewer dead draws because passives are always useful

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon quality is paramount** — mastery multiplies weapon stats
- Swords or Maces with good base damage
- Honor runeword (Tir+El+Amn): +7 dmg, +2 guard, +2 merc attack

## Boss Strategy

Outlast through consistent pressure and defense. Don't need burst windows — steady damage over many turns. Strong against attrition-style bosses.

## Failure Modes

- Too steady, not enough payoff against burst-heavy bosses
- Weapon progression stalls = mastery buffs have nothing to multiply
- Needs some offensive cards from combat tree to have kill pressure
