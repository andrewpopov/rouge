## Identity

The safest build in the game. Defensive auras provide persistent protection for the entire party. Prayer heals, Cleansing removes debuffs, Meditation restores energy, Redemption reclaims from corpses.

## Key Cards

- prayer — Passive healing aura
- resist_fire / resist_cold / resist_lightning — Elemental resistance
- defiance — Guard aura
- cleansing — Debuff removal
- vigor — Speed/efficiency
- meditation — Energy recovery
- redemption — Corpse-based healing
- salvation — Ultimate multi-resist

## Synergy Loop

1. Defensive auras provide persistent party-wide benefits
2. Prayer/meditation tick every turn without card plays
3. Cleansing counters enemy debuff pressure
4. Resistance auras neutralize elemental encounters
5. Combat support cards provide minimum offense

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Maces or Swords — weapon damage less important than usual
- **Paladin Shields** maximize this build's defensive identity
- **Defensive equipment** stacks with aura bonuses

## Boss Strategy

Outheal and out-defend boss damage. Longest fights of any build but highest survival rate. Counter boss elemental attacks with matching resistance auras.

## Failure Modes

- Can stall on damage against high-HP bosses
- Needs some combat skill picks to have kill pressure
- Over-investing in defense with zero offense leads to timeout losses
