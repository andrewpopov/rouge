## Identity

Straightforward melee fighter with aura-enhanced output and defense. Reliable damage + some defense. The most direct Paladin path.

## Key Cards

- smite — Shield-based attack
- zeal — Multi-hit melee
- charge — Gap closer + burst
- vengeance — Elemental melee
- blessed_hammer — Ranged magic damage
- holy_bolt — Holy damage
- fist_of_the_heavens — Ultimate holy attack

## Synergy Loop

1. Zeal provides multi-hit melee pressure
2. Offensive aura support (might, concentration) amplifies damage
3. Defensive aura support (defiance) provides guard
4. Balanced between offense and defense each turn
5. Blessed_hammer adds ranged option for backline targets

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Maces or Swords. Black runeword (Thul+Io+Nef) for maces: +9 dmg
- **Paladin Shields** add unique defensive bonuses

## Boss Strategy

Balance offense and defense each turn. Don't pure-race — use guard and healing auras to survive burst, then counter-attack.

## Failure Modes

- Pure offense without aura defense gets overwhelmed
- Needs weapon progression for damage scaling
- Less ceiling than specialized aura builds but more reliable floor
