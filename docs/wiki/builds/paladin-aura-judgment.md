## Identity

Offensive auras amplify all damage from hero, mercenary, and summons. Holy elemental auras deal passive damage. Conviction strips enemy resistances. The most synergy-dependent Paladin path — needs the aura engine running.

## Key Cards

- might — Damage buff aura
- holy_fire → holy_freeze → holy_shock — Elemental aura chain
- blessed_aim — Accuracy buff
- concentration — Damage focus
- sanctuary — Anti-undead
- fanaticism — Ultimate offensive aura
- conviction — Resistance stripping

## Synergy Loop

1. Activate offensive aura (might, concentration, fanaticism)
2. All allied damage is amplified by aura
3. Holy elemental auras deal passive damage each turn
4. Conviction strips resistances, enabling elemental damage
5. Defensive aura support provides minimum protection

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Maces or Swords. Damage bonuses multiply with aura amplification
- **Focus on damage** — aura provides base amplification, weapon provides raw numbers

## Boss Strategy

Activate strongest aura for the matchup. Fanaticism for general damage. Conviction against resistant bosses. Holy Freeze for control. Aura choice is the key strategic decision.

## Failure Modes

- Synergy-dependent — without aura running, output is mediocre
- Needs defensive aura splashes to survive
- Aura slots are competitive — can only run one at a time
