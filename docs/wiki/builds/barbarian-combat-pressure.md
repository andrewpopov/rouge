## Identity

The default-feeling Barbarian lane. Hit hard, keep fights short, draft just enough defense. Should still require restraint — not "attack every turn."

## Synergy Loop

1. Buff next attack with axe_mastery/increased_stamina (+3 per hit)
2. Follow with bash (14 dmg) or frenzy (10+9 dmg) for amplified burst
3. Stun locks high-threat enemies while cycling damage
4. Tight deck ensures combo pieces recur every 2-3 turns

## Evolution Chains

- bash → stun → concentrate → berserk
- double_swing → frenzy
- leap → leap_attack → whirlwind

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Swords or Maces. Steel runeword (Tir+El) early. Honor (Tir+El+Amn) for mid-game.
- **Weapon quality matters more for Barbarian than most classes**

## Boss Strategy

Draft survival for telegraphed burst turns. This build can't pure-race bosses — needs guard/healing for charge intents.

## Failure Modes

- Pure race plan dies to big boss turns
- Overextending without defense leads to death on counter-attacks
