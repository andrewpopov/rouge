## Identity

Self-sustaining melee fighter. Shapeshifting provides multi-hit attacks and built-in life recovery. Doesn't need external healing — converts damage dealt into survivability.

## Key Cards

- werewolf, lycanthropy — Transformation enablers
- feral_rage — Attack + life steal
- fire_claws — Elemental melee damage
- hunger — Life recovery on hit
- shock_wave — AoE stun utility
- fury — Ultimate multi-hit payoff
- rabies — Poison DoT application

## Synergy Loop

1. Transform for stat bonuses
2. Multi-hit attacks (feral_rage, fury) deal damage and heal
3. Each hit converts damage → life through sustain mechanics
4. Summoning support provides additional bodies and buffs

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Maces or Polearms — Earthsong runeword (Ral+Sol+Io) for maces/staves
- **Polearms** offer slow on hit for control during melee

## Boss Strategy

Sustain through boss fights. Self-healing means long fights favor this build. Multi-hit attacks stack up against high-HP targets.

## Failure Modes

- Multiple status-applying enemies can overwhelm self-healing
- No ranged answer — vulnerable to backline enemies while in melee
- Needs summoning support for bodies; solo shifter is too exposed
