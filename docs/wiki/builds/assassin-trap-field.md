## Identity

Deploy persistent trap fields that deal ongoing damage. Traps are "set and forget" — they deal damage each turn while the Assassin focuses on defense and positioning. Tax-style pressure that accumulates over the fight.

## Key Cards

- fire_blast — Basic fire trap
- charged_bolt_sentry — Lightning trap
- wake_of_inferno — Fire persistent area
- lightning_sentry — Primary damage trap
- death_sentry — Corpse detonation trap (chain reactions)
- blade_shield — Passive melee deterrent

## Synergy Loop

1. Deploy traps early (setup phase)
2. Traps deal damage each turn passively
3. Death Sentry chain-detonates corpses for escalating damage
4. Shadow support provides defense while traps kill
5. Value increases every turn traps are active (scaling)

## Boss Strategy

Get traps deployed early and focus on survival. Trap damage accumulates over time — longer fights favor this build more than burst. Shadow defense buys time.

## Failure Modes

- Weak to fast openers that kill before traps activate
- Setup time required — first 2-3 turns are defensive
- AoE enemy attacks can overwhelm before trap value pays off
