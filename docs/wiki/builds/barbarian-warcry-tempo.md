## Identity

The most "deckbuilder" Barbarian lane. Uses buffs, taunts, guard, and party control to create timing windows where buff uptime outperforms raw stat racing. Sequencing and mercenary coordination matter.

## Key Cards

- howl → shout → battle_orders → war_cry — Warcry progression
- battle_command — Party-wide buff
- battle_cry — Enemy debuff
- taunt — Threat redirect
- find_potion — Heal + utility

## Synergy Loop

1. Shout/battle_orders provide persistent guard and stat buffs
2. Battle_cry debuffs enemies, reducing incoming damage
3. Howl disrupts enemy intent timing
4. Mercenary benefits from buff stacking (attack bonuses)
5. Timing windows where buffs overlap create dominant turns

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Maces preferred (warcry synergy), Swords as fallback
- **Defensive equipment** matters more here than raw weapon damage
- Black runeword (Thul+Io+Nef): +9 dmg, +4 guard, +6 life for maces

## Boss Strategy

Survive long enough for buff windows to outperform raw stat racing. Stack warcry effects, then attack during buff uptime. Patient, timing-based boss approach.

## Failure Modes

- Can drift into low-damage stall — needs some combat skill picks
- Pure buff stacking without damage leads to timeout-style losses
- Mana-intensive — vulnerable to energy drain if buffs can't be maintained
