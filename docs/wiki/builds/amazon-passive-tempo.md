## Identity

The "play clean and scale smart" lane. Wins through efficiency, dodges, crit-style tempo, and mercenary support. Not about explosive damage — about never wasting a turn.

## Key Cards

- critical_strike, deadly_strike, pierce — damage scaling chain
- dodge, avoid, evade — defensive scaling chain
- inner_sight, penetrate, valkyrie — support/utility chain
- inner_calm — heal + guard + next ranged buff

## Synergy Loop

1. Passive investment buffs every attack without costing energy
2. Critical_strike chain scales damage per hit
3. Dodge chain provides "free" defense without card plays
4. Mercenary support through marks amplifies ally damage
5. Efficient enough to win fights without dedicated burst turns

## Gear Priorities

- **Weapon:** Any Amazon weapon family (Bow, Javelin, Spear) — build is weapon-flexible
- **Focus on defense:** This build relies on scaling, not raw weapon power

## Boss Strategy

Make sure the deck still has a real boss answer. Passive scaling alone doesn't close games against high-HP bosses. Need at least one damage payoff card.

## Failure Modes

- Lacks a real finisher if damage never scales high enough
- Passive bonuses accumulate slowly — vulnerable in early acts
- No dedicated AoE answer for swarm encounters
