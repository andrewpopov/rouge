## Strategy Notes

- **Quest bosses** serve as difficulty checks for each act zone
- **Minibosses** are harder than quest bosses but have better rewards
- **Council fight** (Act 3) is a multi-boss encounter requiring AoE and priority targeting
- **The Ancients** (Act 5) is the hardest non-final boss fight — 3 warriors with different abilities
- **Nihlathak** is dangerous due to Corpse Explosion — can chain-reaction off your kills
