## Act 5 Progression Notes

- **Every enemy has at least 1 affix** — no "clean" fights remain
- **Elites roll 2-3 affixes** — expect compound threat combinations
- The Ancients (3v1) is the hardest non-final boss fight
- Baal's Throne Waves serve as an endurance check before the final boss
- Nihlathak side boss is optional but extremely dangerous (Corpse Explosion chains)
- Cold-enchanted enemies are common — cold resistance/immunity valuable
- Baal's Mana Burn punishes high-energy builds (Sorceress beware)
- Final skill check — deck must handle everything: AoE, single-target, defense, status answers
- Guest enemies from Acts 1-4 appear in Nightmare/Hell difficulty
