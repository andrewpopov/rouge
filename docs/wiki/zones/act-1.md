## Side Branches

### Den of Evil (from Blood Moor)
- 5 encounters, battle type
- Completion triggers Deck Surgery unlock

### Burial Grounds (from Cold Plains)
- 3 encounters, miniboss type
- Boss: **Blood Raven** — corrupt rogue archer, summons skeletons

### Tristram (from Stony Field, gated by Dark Wood)
- 4 encounters, battle type
- Boss: **Griswold** — corrupted blacksmith
- Completion triggers Deck Surgery unlock

### Forgotten Tower (from Black Marsh)
- 5 encounters, miniboss type
- Boss: **The Countess** — fire enchanted, rune drops

## Deck Surgery Unlock Zones

Clearing any of these unlocks one-time card removal at the Quartermaster:
- Den of Evil
- Burial Grounds / Black Pit
- Tristram
- Ashfall Hamlet

## Mercenary Available

**Blackwood Hunter** (Rogue Scout) — Mark hunter, Precision aura
Variants: Blackwood Tracker, Blackwood Sentinel

## Act 1 Progression Notes

- This is the tutorial act — decks are small, fights are forgiving
- Focus on establishing archetype direction by end of Act 1
- Weapon family should be chosen by Cold Plains/Stony Field area
- Den of Evil side branch is worth clearing for Deck Surgery access
- Andariel is fire-weak (-50%) — fire damage trivializes the fight
