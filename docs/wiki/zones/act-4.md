# Act 4 — The Harrowing

> Pandemonium Fortress through Chaos Sanctuary. Boss: Diablo.

Last updated: 2026-04-11

## Town: Pandemonium Fortress

NPCs: Tyrael (Quest), Halbu (Blacksmith), Jamella (Vendor)

## Mainline Zones (5)

| Zone | Encounters | Type | Enemy Highlights |
|------|-----------|------|-----------------|
| Outer Steppes | 1-2 | Battle | Finger Mages, Megademons |
| Plains of Despair | 1-2 | Battle | Megademons, Oblivion Knights |
| City of the Damned | 2-3 | Battle | Vile Mothers (death_spawn), dense packs |
| River of Flame | 2-3 | Battle | **Hephasto** (quest boss), fire-heavy enemies |
| Chaos Sanctuary | 3+ | Boss | Seal guardians → **Diablo** |

## No Side Branches

Act 4 is the shortest act with no optional zones. Every encounter is mandatory.

## Chaos Sanctuary Special

The Chaos Sanctuary has a unique structure:
1. Clear three seal guardian bosses (Grand Vizier, Lord De Seis, Infector of Souls)
2. Each seal guardian has their own pack
3. After all three seals are broken, Diablo spawns
4. This is effectively a 4-boss act finale

## Act 4 Progression Notes

- **Elites now roll 2-3 affixes** — compound threat combinations are common
- Normal enemies can roll 0-1 affix
- Oblivion Knights cast curses (Iron Maiden, Decrepify) — dangerous for melee builds
- Vile Mothers use death_spawn — killing them spawns Vile Children
- River of Flame is the last chance to heal/prepare before Diablo
- Diablo is the hardest non-final boss — fire + lightning dual element threat
- Shortest act but highest density of dangerous encounters
