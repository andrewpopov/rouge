## Acts and Mainline Progression

### Act I - The Sightless Eye (Boss: Andariel)
- Mainline route: Rogue Encampment -> Blood Moor -> Cold Plains -> Stony Field -> The Underground Passage -> Dark Wood -> Black Marsh -> Tamoe Highland -> Monastery Gate -> Outer Cloister -> Barracks -> Jail -> Inner Cloister -> Cathedral -> Catacombs -> Andariel.
- Common side/quest zones: Burial Grounds, Tristram.

### Act II - The Secret of the Vizjerei (Boss: Duriel)
- Mainline route: Lut Gholein -> Rocky Waste -> Dry Hills/Far Oasis/Lost City quest branches -> Harem -> The Palace Cellar -> Arcane Sanctuary -> Canyon of the Magi -> Tal Rasha's Tomb -> Tal Rasha's Chamber -> Duriel.
- Common side/quest zones: Sewers, Halls of the Dead, Maggot Lair, Valley of Snakes.

### Act III - The Infernal Gate (Boss: Mephisto)
- Mainline route: Kurast Docks -> Spider Forest -> Great Marsh/Flayer Jungle branch -> Lower Kurast -> Kurast Bazaar -> Upper Kurast -> Kurast Causeway -> Travincal -> Durance of Hate -> Mephisto.
- Common side/quest zones: Spider Cavern, Flayer Dungeon, Sewers.

### Act IV - The Harrowing (Boss: Diablo)
- Mainline route: Pandemonium Fortress -> Outer Steppes -> Plains of Despair -> City of the Damned -> River of Flame -> Chaos Sanctuary -> Diablo.
- Side content is limited compared to other acts.

### Act V - Lord of Destruction (Boss: Baal)
- Mainline route: Harrogath -> Bloody Foothills -> Frigid Highlands -> Arreat Plateau -> Crystalline Passage -> Glacial Trail -> Frozen Tundra -> The Ancients' Way -> Arreat Summit -> Worldstone Keep -> Throne of Destruction -> The Worldstone Chamber -> Baal.
- Common side/quest zones: Nihlathak's Temple, Halls of Anguish, Halls of Pain, Halls of Vaught, Frozen River, Abaddon, Pit of Acheron, Drifter Cavern, Infernal Pit, Icy Cellar.
